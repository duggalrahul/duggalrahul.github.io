Rahul Duggal

I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology where I am co-advised by Prof. Polo Chau (from Georgia Tech) and Prof. Jimeng Sun (from University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign).

My current research interests lie in the area of robust and efficient deep learning with applications in the areas of healthcare, fashion retrieval and face verification.
